About our office

Aaja Ko Khabar

Aajakokhabar.com is one of the most chosen news portals throughout the nation. Successfully running since 7 years, Aajako Khabar was officially registered as Aajako Khabar Media Pvt. Ltd. in 2015 from Registrar Office. There are almost 1.8 million people reading Aajako Khabar in Facebook. Almost hundred thousand visitors visit the portal in a day. Almost 20 professionals are involved including working journalists and other staff. Aajako Khabar has been providing fresh and fact news since its establishment. Conserving Nepali Literature and Language along with promoting the Nepali Online Journalism is the motive of Aajako Khabar. Aajako Khabar believes that the sovereignty of news is greater than anything in this world. The aim of Aajako Khabar is to prolong the role of media house with maintaining the diversity in languages and standard of the news.
